We position this work at the intersection of robust machine learning, sensor fusion, and neuro-symbolic integration. Traditional probabilistic filtering approaches (e.g., Kalman filtering) provide principled state estimation for low-dimensional telemetry but do not operate directly on raw images \cite{kalman1960new,thrun2005probabilistic}. End-to-end deep sensor-fusion models can learn rich representations but are vulnerable to OOD failures \cite{goodfellow2016deep}. Neuro-symbolic methods aim to combine the strengths of symbolic reasoning with neural perception; foundational treatments include \cite{garcez2009neural}.
